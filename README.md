## 💡 Content Strategy Framework

**Content Pillars:**
- 30% Product Showcase & New Releases
- 25% Style Tips & Styling Combinations
- 20% Brand Story & Behind-the-Scenes
- 15% User-Generated Content & Customer Stories
- 10% Trending/Entertainment & Fun Content

## 📈 Social Media Calendar Structure

**Daily Posting:**
- 1-3 posts per day across platforms
- Stories/Reels for daily engagement
- DM responses and community interaction

**Weekly Structure:**
- Monday: Motivation/Inspiration
- Tuesday: Style Tips & Tutorials
- Wednesday: Behind-the-Scenes
- Thursday: Customer Spotlight/UGC
- Friday: New Product Showcase
- Saturday: Trending/Entertainment
- Sunday: Community Engagement

## 🎯 Engagement Strategies

- **Response Time**: Reply to comments within 24 hours
- **Story Engagement**: 2-3 Stories daily with interactive elements
- **Reel Strategy**: Trending audio, trending formats, entertaining content
- **Community Building**: Feature user content, build relationships
- **Hashtag Strategy**: Mix of popular, niche, and branded hashtags
